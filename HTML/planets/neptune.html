Neptune

Neptune, the planet, is a deep azure blue, thanks largely to methane in its atmosphere absorbing red light. It’s the eighth planet from the Sun, orbiting every ~165 years.Neptune’s vivid blue and dynamic weather draw an instant parallel to the sea. In fact, Neptune is named “for the Roman god of the sea, and the deep, ocean blue color of the planet reflects this” Neptune in astrology is associated with dreams, intuition, spirituality, and illusion.
