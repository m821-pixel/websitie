Saturn

Saturn is the second-largest planet in our solar system and is instantly recognizable by its spectacular ring system. Saturn's rings are believed to have formed from the debris of comets, asteroids, or moons that broke up in orbit around Saturn, torn apart by the planet's powerful gravity.

Saturnus (Saturn) was a complex Roman god, originally of agriculture – particularly sowing seeds – and later associated with time and renewal. Saturn is associated with limitations, responsibilities, and structure. It represents discipline, authority, and lessons learned over time. Human beings are social… We are born into familial relationships and build many other relationships as we grow. Each relationship requires effort to foster.
