Jupiter

Jupiter is the giant of the solar system, more massive than all the other planets combined. Its diameter is about 143,000 km (11 Earths across). This enormity aligns perfectly with the idea of Jupiter as the most grand and expansive deity. Jupiter (Latin Iuppiter, also called Jove) was the chief deity of Roman religion – the king of the gods, god of the sky and thunder. He wielded the thunderbolt as his weapon and oversaw laws and social order.
