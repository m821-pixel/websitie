Venus- The goddess of beauty; Romans at that time knew nothing about the truth of Venus, the planet similar to the size of the Earth but holding a harsh, hostile world with intense heat and pressure. All the Romans could see at that point in time was a bright star in the sky: a planet that reflects 70% of the sunlight due to its thick clouds and hence shines brightly in the night sky. This radiant planet, beautiful to the eyes stargazing on a dark night, was named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty, Venus.
